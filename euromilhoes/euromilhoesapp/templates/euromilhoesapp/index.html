Use AI to predict lottery numbers

A lottery numbers' generator uses AI inteligence to analyze lottary statistics and predict on that basis the numbers of the next closest drawing.

1

Study the plots

In the diagrams section, you can check and get some understanding on the dependencies of the number distribution of factors such as time, day of the week, and the order in which the numbers are falling out.

2

Choose args

The statistics' section contains all numerical data from the draws since 2004. Here you can select the data that will be fed as arguments to the neural network for analysis.

3

Choose AI

In the neural network' section, you can select one of two neural networks to simulate winning numbers. Both networks are almost equal in accuracy, but produce different results.
